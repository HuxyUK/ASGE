6 /*******************************************************************************
7  * https://gist.github.com/kevinkreiser/39f2e39273c625d96790
8  *
9  * This is a thread safe cross platform logger. It's versatile in that it
10  * can be extended to support additional logger's i.e. xml, json as well as
11  * coming with a std::cout and basic file logger.
12  *
13  * It works well enough, however, logging can have a negative impact on
14  * performance, so choose a log level that best fits the build you're producing.
15  * For example a release build may disable all logging to reduce the impact
16  * logging would have.
17  *
18  * -DLOGGING_LEVEL_NONE to disable logging OR
19  * target_compile_definitions(asge PUBLIC LOGGING_LEVEL_NONE)
20  *
21  * The default logging uses cout with colours. You must configure alternatives
22  * logging::configure({ {"type", "file"}, {"file_name", "test.log"}, {"reopen_interval", "1"} });
23  *
24  * Some predefined logging functions/levels are provided
25  * logging::ERROR("logging error");
26  * logging::WARN("logging warning");
27  * logging::INFO(logging info)
28  * logging::DEBUG(logging debug)
29  * logging::TRACE(logging trace)
30  * logging::log(logging::timestamp() + " \x1b[35;1m[CUSTOM]\x1b[0m " + "msg");
31  *
32  ******************************************************************************/
